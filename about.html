An editorial look into our roastery, farmers, and tasting notes.

Our Roastery

At Aurum we believe a great cup begins long before the roast. We partner with farmers who tend small lots, document the lot provenance, and share tasting insights in every bag. Our roastery is purpose-built for small-batch precision — we roast in measured lots, track roast profiles, and prioritize freshness.

Traceable sourcing

We visit origin farms, record farmer stories, and display that information on our bags. Transparency helps you taste terroir — and helps farmers receive fair pay.

Sustainability & craft

From compostable packaging to energy-efficient roasters, we choose processes that protect flavor and reduce impact. Our team runs cuppings weekly so every roast meets our tasting standards.
